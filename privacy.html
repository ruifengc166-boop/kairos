Privacy & Beta Terms

KAIROS is an early symbolic timing product. It reads timing, not fate. This page explains how beta submissions are handled.

What we collect

When you submit a Personal Timing Signal request, we may collect your email address, your question, selected area, recent signal, emotional state, time window, question timestamp, timezone, current Eastern hour layer, Western lunar layer, and Today’s Timing Almanac context. Birth details are optional.

How we use it

We use your submission to prepare or evaluate a KAIROS timing reply, improve the product experience, understand beta usage patterns, and contact you about your submitted request.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your submission data.
  • We do not publicly display your personal question without permission.
  • We do not use KAIROS as medical, legal, financial, or emergency advice.
  • We do not claim to predict fate or guarantee outcomes.

Optional birth details

If you provide birth date, birth time, or birth place, those fields are treated as optional symbolic context for deeper readings. You can leave them blank.

Beta limitation

KAIROS is currently an early reader beta. Today’s Timing Almanac and Quick Timing Check are symbolic reflection tools. They are not full traditional almanac calculations, deterministic predictions, or replacements for practical judgment.

Deletion requests

You may request deletion of your submitted information by contacting hello@readkairos.com.
